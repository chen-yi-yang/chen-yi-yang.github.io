Assistant Professor & Sociolinguist

(by ChatGPT, TBD) 我對社會語言學領域感興趣，特別是其中的語言使用與轉變、語言維持與流失、語言態度與身份認同、語言復興、語言政策與規劃等子領域。我認為語言不僅是溝通的工具，更是反映不同文化、社會與歷史背景的媒介。每種語言都有其獨特性，應該被保存並傳承下去。

I am interested in the field of sociolinguistics, especially in the subfield, such as language use and shift, language maintenance and loss, language attitude and identity, language revitalization, language policy and planning, etc. I think that language is not only a tool for communication, but also a medium to reflect different cultures, societies and historical backgrounds. Every language has its own uniqueness and should be preserved and passed down.
